State Central Library


State Central library, Trivandrum

The Trivandrum Public Library (State Central Library) is one of the oldest Libraries in India. It was established in the year 1829 A.D. during the reign of His Highness Sree Swathi Thirunal Maharaja of Travancore. The work of starting and organising the Library was entrusted to Col.Edward Cadogan, then the British Resident who was the grand son of Sir Hans Sloan, the founder of the British Museum.
